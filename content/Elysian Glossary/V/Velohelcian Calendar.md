The calendar established by [[Divinity#Velohelcia Tyrread, Symbol of Order|Velohelcia Tyrread]], [[Symbol]] of Order, consisting of tenday weeks and 40-day months.

*In order, its months are:*
- Stella Ortus,  *Faint [[Starfall]]*
- Secunda,  *Faint Autumn*
- Tertia,  *Faint Winter*
- Quarta,  *Bright Spring*
- Quinta,  *Bright Summer*
- Stella Occasus,  *Bright [[Starfall]]*
- Septima,  *Bright Autumn*
- Octava,  *Bright Winter*
- Novena,  *Faint Spring*
- Decima,  *Faint Summer*
