# Welcome to Elysium, Voyager
**Elysium** is a is a sword-and-sorcery science-fantasy world of floating islands adrift over the endless [[Elysian Glossary#Ivory Sea|Ivory Sea]]. In Elysium, skyships and beasts soar the skies between industrialized cities, drifting frontier shards, and ancient ruins coveting lost arcana, glittering hoards, and lethal consequences.
