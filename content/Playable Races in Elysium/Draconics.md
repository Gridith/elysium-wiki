#####  ***DESCENDED FROM DRAGONS***
Also known as dragonblood or wyrmwights, draconics are humanoids with draconic features such as full-body or partial scales, wings, or an elementally charged breath. All draconics bear hard scales known as Wyrmplate, ranging from full-body plating to scattered potions. When a dragonblood has children, they are almost always draconics themselves.
#####  ***AS VIBRANT AS VARIED***
Their scales are colored dependent on their lineage, such and the scales of the original wyrm(s). The offspring of draconics of differing coloring will have a mix of their colors. Because of this vast variety in hues, there is no set list of scale colors. Most draconics choose a term or epithet they think fits the appearance of their own scales the best.
#####  ***A CULTURE OF HONOUR***
Much of draconic culture has a dedication to honour. Draconics tend to view all living things, even hated enemies, as deserving of courtesy and respect. Cowardice is not simply undesirable among dragonblood, it is considered outright repulsive.
#####  ***NON-PATRONYMIC NAMES***
A typical draconic has a single name, as many of them see a surname as a means to unfairly claim honour through your family. Different wyrmwight cultures have vastly different naming conventions beyond this, such as many of them traditionally changing their name upon reaching adulthood.
