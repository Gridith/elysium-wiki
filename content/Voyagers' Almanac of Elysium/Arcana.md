>[!abstract] The Nature of Magic and the Supernatural
>In Elysium, magical and fantastical things echo throughout almost every aspect of life. From the foundational esotericisms upholding the [[Bastions|bastions]] to the radiance inherit to [[Symbols|symbols]], these supernatural phenomena are referred to as [[content/Voyagers' Almanac of Elysium/Arcana|arcana]].
>
>Some common examples of major [[content/Voyagers' Almanac of Elysium/Arcana|arcana]] include the scholastic study of thaumaturgists and wizards, the light conducted through faith in the ordained and [[Symbols|symbols]], or the deep-rooted beliefs and traditions upheld by generations of commonfolk.
