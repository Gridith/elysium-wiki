>[!abstract] The Nature of Bastions and the Sea
> The landmasses on the [[Ivory Sea]] are known as Bastions. Any sufficiently large structure can be reasonably referred to as a bastion, regardless of if it’s natural or manmade.
> 
> Some bastions, like the [[Black Iron Clasp]] or [[The Sunken Bastion|The Mirrodin Bastion]] are simply known by a settlement upon them, especially if it’s the only settlement. Others are typically referred to by the names the locals have given them, such as the landmass making up the [[Dawnwind Peninsula]] or an uninhabited speeding rock named [[Elintrea’s Comet]].
