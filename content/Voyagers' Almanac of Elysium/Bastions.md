>[!abstract] The Nature of islands and Bastions
> In Elysium, the land is broken into floating islands that drift through the sky along distinct and often peculiar courses. Some islands maintain steady, cyclical paths, while others show no consistent pattern at all, and the force that keeps them naturally afloat is an active field of study.
> 
> Any sufficiently large landmass can be reasonably referred to as a [[Bastion|bastion]], regardless of if it’s natural or manmade. Some bastions, like the [[Black Iron Clasp]] are simply known by a settlement upon them, especially if it’s the only settlement. Others are typically referred to by the names the locals have given them, such as the landmass making up the [[Dawnwind Peninsula]] or an uninhabited speeding rock named [[Elintrea’s Comet]].
