# Faint and Bright Seasons

# The [[Velohelcian Calendar|Common Calendar]]
There are very few civilizations or communities in [[Elysium]] that don't use the [[Velohelcian Calendar]], merchants and [[Voyager of Fortune|voyagers]] alike prefer it for it's simply structure of tenday weeks and neatly divided 40-day months. Although some critique the calendar for simplifying too much, such as it's subtly inaccurate seasons.
