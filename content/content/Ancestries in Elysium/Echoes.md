##### Connected to the Symbolic
People who have been changed by the powers of a [[content/Voyagers' Almanac of Elysium/Divinity\|Symbol]] are called Echoes, such as descendants of [[content/Voyagers' Almanac of Elysium/Divinity\|Symbol]] like the [[content/Voyagers' Almanac of Elysium/Divinity#Aureltyren Vorell - Symbol of Artisanry\|Vorell Family]], or for instance the high priests of the [[content/Elysian Glossary/V/Velohelcian Theocracy]] who have become Echoes through devotion to a [[content/Voyagers' Almanac of Elysium/Divinity\|Symbol]].
