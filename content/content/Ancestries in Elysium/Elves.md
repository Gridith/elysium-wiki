##### Pale Glamours
Elves are a species infused with a supernatural capability called [[content/Elysian Glossary/G/Glamour\|glamours]], which they are innately familiar with. While most [[content/Elysian Glossary/G/Glamour\|glamours]] are permanent from birth, some elves have seen their [[content/Elysian Glossary/G/Glamour\|glamours]] change over time. Some [[content/Elysian Glossary/G/Glamour\|glamours]] may make others perceive an elf as more interesting or engaging, while others can allow an elf to see their surroundings even in complete darkness.
##### Graceful Physique
Elves tend to possess graceful, slender physiques, usually reaching a height of about six feet. Their skin and hair are known for being as pale as the [[content/Elysian Glossary/I/Ivory Sea]] itself, though their hair can somewhat uncommonly be seen in darker shades as well.
##### Patient Perspective
Relative to the other species in [[content/Elysian Glossary/E/Elysium]], elves enjoy a rather long lifespan of a 200 to 300 years, giving them a more patient but sometimes distant perspective. An elf who is pursuing a goal is often immensely focused and diligent.
