# Welcome to [[Glossary#Elysium|Elysium]], [[Glossary#Voyager of Fortune|Voyager]]
**[[Glossary#Elysium|Elysium]]** is a sword-and-sorcery science-fantasy world of floating islands adrift over the endless [[Glossary#Ivory Sea|Ivory Sea]]. In [[Glossary#Elysium|Elysium]], skyships and beasts soar the skies between industrialized cities, drifting frontier [[Glossary#Shard|shards]], and ancient ruins coveting lost [[Glossary#Arcana|Arcana]], glittering hoards, and lethal consequences.
